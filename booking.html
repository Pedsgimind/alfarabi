Book Your Appointment Online

Please use this page to request an appointment with Al Farabi Gastroenterology & Endoscopy Centre. Our team will review your request and contact you by phone, WhatsApp, or email to confirm the date and time. For urgent or emergency problems, go directly to the nearest emergency department.

Online Appointment Request Form

Fill in the form below as completely as you can. Do not include highly sensitive personal information. This form is for non-urgent appointment requests only.

After submitting this form, you should receive an email confirmation from Avicenna Medical Inc. Our staff will contact you as soon as possible to confirm the exact appointment date and time.

Need urgent help?

If you are experiencing severe pain, high fever, heavy bleeding, difficulty breathing, confusion, or any other emergency symptoms, please go to the nearest emergency department or call your local emergency services immediately.

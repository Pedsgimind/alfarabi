Clinic services

Al Farabi Centre provides coordinated adult and pediatric digestive health care, with on-site endoscopy and access to supporting specialties including internal medicine, pediatrics, dermatology, and surgery.

Evaluation and management of gastrointestinal and liver disorders in adults.

  • Reflux, peptic ulcer disease, and dyspepsia.
  • Chronic abdominal pain, bloating, and bowel habit changes.
  • Inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) and irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
  • Liver disease including fatty liver, viral hepatitis, and cirrhosis.
  • Gastrointestinal bleeding and anemia workup.

üë∂Pediatric GI, liver & nutrition

Subspecialty care for infants, children, and adolescents with digestive and nutrition concerns.

  • Celiac disease and malabsorption disorders.
  • Constipation, reflux, and functional abdominal pain.
  • Inflammatory bowel disease in children.
  • Feeding difficulties, growth faltering, and obesity.
  • Chronic liver, pancreatic, and biliary disorders.

On-site diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopy performed by trained endoscopists.

  • Upper endoscopy (EGD) and colonoscopy.

  • Foreign body removal and esophageal dilatation.
  • Percutaneous endoscopic gastrostomy (PEG) placement and care.
  • Screening and surveillance for colorectal cancer.

ü§ùAllied and support services

On-site or closely linked services to support comprehensive gastrointestinal care.

  • On-site pharmacy and laboratory services.
  • Coordination with regional hospitals for advanced imaging and interventions.

Detailed availability and scheduling for each service may vary. Please contact the clinic or your referring physician for the most up-to-date information.

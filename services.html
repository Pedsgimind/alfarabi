Clinic Services

Al Farabi Gastroenterology & Endoscopy Centre provides integrated adult and pediatric services, including outpatient consultations, diagnostic workup, and therapeutic endoscopy, supported by on-site pharmacy and laboratory.

Adult

Adult Gastroenterology & Hepatology

Comprehensive assessment and management of:

  • Reflux, peptic ulcer disease, dyspepsia and functional disorders.
  • Inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) and irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
  • Chronic liver disease, viral hepatitis, cirrhosis and portal hypertension.
  • Biliary and pancreatic disorders, including gallstones and pancreatitis.
Pediatric

Pediatric Gastroenterology, Hepatology & Nutrition

Care for infants, children and adolescents with:

  • Chronic abdominal pain, constipation, diarrhea and vomiting.
  • Growth faltering, malnutrition, obesity and feeding difficulties.
  • Chronic liver disease, cholestasis, metabolic and genetic liver disorders.
  • Celiac disease, food allergy, inflammatory bowel disease, GI bleeding.
Endoscopy

Diagnostic & Therapeutic Endoscopy

On-site endoscopy suite providing:

  • Upper endoscopy and colonoscopy for adults and children.
  • Polypectomy, variceal band ligation, foreign body removal.
  • Esophageal dilatation, PEG/PEC placement and management.
  • Urgent endoscopy for GI bleeding and high-risk foreign bodies.
Multidisciplinary

General Pediatrics & Internal Medicine

Supporting general medical care including:

  • Common pediatric and adult medical problems.
  • Chronic disease follow-up, hypertension, diabetes, anemia.
  • Pre-operative assessment and post-hospital discharge review.
Surgical & Dermatology

General / GI Surgery & Dermatology

On-site consultants providing:

  • Evaluation for gallbladder, hernia and colorectal surgery.
  • Skin and hair disorders, rashes, acne and chronic dermatoses.
  • Joint planning of cases requiring combined GI and surgical care.
Support

Pharmacy, Laboratory & Coordination

Support services include:

  • On-site pharmacy for essential medications and prescriptions.
  • Laboratory coordination for blood tests and imaging referrals.
  • Electronic visit summaries and communication with referring physicians.

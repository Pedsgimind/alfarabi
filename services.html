Clinical Services

Al Farabi Center offers coordinated care for adults and children with digestive and general medical problems. Services are usually provided by appointment, with some flexibility based on physician schedules.

Adult Gastroenterology & Hepatology

  • Evaluation of upper and lower GI symptoms (reflux, pain, bleeding, diarrhea, constipation)
  • Assessment of chronic liver disease and abnormal liver tests
  • Endoscopic diagnosis and treatment where indicated

Clinic services

Al Farabi Centre provides coordinated adult and pediatric digestive health care, with on-site endoscopy and access to supporting specialties including internal medicine, pediatrics, dermatology, and surgery.

Our Clinical Services

Avicenna Medical Inc. provides comprehensive, collaborative care for adults and children with digestive, liver, nutrition, skin, surgical, and general health needs. Below is an overview of the main service groups and examples of conditions we assess and manage.

Adult Gastroenterology & Hepatology

Evaluation and management of digestive and liver diseases in adults, including:

  • Acid reflux, heartburn, and gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD).
  • Peptic ulcer disease, gastritis, and Helicobacter pylori infection.
  • Difficulty swallowing (dysphagia), esophageal strictures, and motility disorders.
  • Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and functional bowel disorders.
  • Inflammatory bowel disease (IBD): Crohn’s disease and ulcerative colitis.
  • Chronic diarrhea, constipation, bloating, and abdominal pain.
  • Screening and surveillance colonoscopy for polyps and colorectal cancer.
  • Investigation of gastrointestinal bleeding and iron deficiency anemia.
  • Fatty liver disease (NAFLD/NASH) and alcohol-related liver disease.
  • Chronic viral hepatitis (when applicable by local guidelines and referral pathways).
  • Cirrhosis, portal hypertension, and complications such as ascites or varices.
  • Pancreatic disorders, including acute and chronic pancreatitis.
  • Gallbladder and biliary tract problems (e.g., gallstones, biliary colic).

Pediatric Gastroenterology, Hepatology & Nutrition

Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents with digestive, liver, feeding, and nutrition problems, including:

  • Chronic abdominal pain, vomiting, reflux, and feeding difficulties.
  • Constipation, encopresis, chronic diarrhea, and stooling problems.
  • Failure to thrive, poor weight gain, and growth concerns.
  • Celiac disease and other causes of chronic diarrhea and malabsorption.
  • Food allergies, food intolerance, and eosinophilic gastrointestinal disorders.
  • Functional stomach and bowel disorders (e.g., functional dyspepsia, IBS).
  • Inflammatory bowel disease in children (Crohn’s disease and ulcerative colitis).
  • Pediatric liver disease, jaundice, and abnormal liver tests.
  • Pancreatic disorders in children, including recurrent pancreatitis.
  • Nutritional assessment and support, including obesity and undernutrition.
  • Feeding team approach for complex feeding and swallowing difficulties.

General Pediatrics

Comprehensive care for infants, children, and adolescents, including:

  • Routine well-child visits and growth and development monitoring.
  • Assessment and management of common childhood infections.
  • Asthma, allergic conditions, and respiratory problems.
  • Headache, abdominal pain, and other common pediatric complaints.
  • Behavior, sleep, and school-related health concerns.
  • Vaccination counseling according to local and international guidelines.
  • Coordination of care for children with complex or chronic medical conditions.

Dermatology

Diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail conditions, such as:

  • Eczema, atopic dermatitis, and contact dermatitis.
  • Acne, rosacea, and other inflammatory skin disorders.
  • Psoriasis and autoimmune-related skin conditions.
  • Fungal, viral, and bacterial skin infections.
  • Pigmented lesions, moles, and skin cancer screening (where applicable).
  • Hair loss, scalp conditions, and nail disorders.

General & Gastrointestinal Surgery

Surgical consultation and procedures for adults and children, including:

  • Gallbladder surgery for gallstones and biliary colic.
  • Hernia repair (inguinal, umbilical, ventral, and others).
  • Appendectomy and emergency abdominal surgery.
  • Colorectal surgery planning and coordination.
  • Management of anorectal problems (fissures, fistulae, hemorrhoids).
  • Minor surgical procedures performed in clinic (where applicable).

Laboratory Services

On-site or affiliated laboratory services to support diagnosis and follow-up, including:

  • Complete blood count, biochemistry, and liver function tests.
  • Inflammatory markers (e.g., CRP, ESR) and anemia workup.
  • Stool tests for infection, inflammation, and malabsorption.
  • Celiac serology and other autoimmune panels.
  • Hormonal and metabolic testing when clinically indicated.

Imaging Services

Imaging is provided on-site or through trusted partner centers, including:

  • Abdominal ultrasound for liver, gallbladder, pancreas, and bowel.
  • Pelvic and renal ultrasound for urinary and gynecological concerns.
  • X-ray imaging (plain radiography) where indicated.
  • Advanced imaging (CT, MRI, MRCP, endoscopic ultrasound) arranged by referral.

Pharmacy Services

Pharmacy support for patients seen in our clinic, including:

  • Medication dispensing according to local regulations.
  • Counseling on proper use of medications and possible side effects.
  • Support with complex treatment plans (e.g., IBD, chronic liver disease).
  • Coordination for special or hard-to-access medications when necessary.

Other Services & Referral-Based Care

Additional services may be offered directly or through trusted referral partners, such as:

  • Nutrition and dietitian consultations for adults and children.
  • Psychology and mental health support for chronic illness and functional disorders.
  • Endocrinology, cardiology, and other medical subspecialties as needed.
  • Pre-operative and post-operative assessments and follow-up.
  • Coordination with hospitals for advanced procedures and inpatient care.

If the service you need is not listed here, please contact us. Our team will guide you to the most appropriate clinic or referral pathway.

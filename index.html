Avicenna Medical INC. proudly launching Al Farabi Gastroenterology Center

Al Farabi Gastroenterology & Endoscopy Center is a multidisciplinary clinic in Baqubah providing adult and pediatric gastroenterology, internal medicine, general pediatrics, general surgery, dermatology and on-site endoscopy – all under one roof.

Why Patients Choose Al Farabi Center

Our team brings together multiple specialties to provide safe, coordinated and convenient care close to home – from common digestive complaints to complex pediatric GI, liver, and nutrition problems.

We work closely with the Diyala Health Ministry and hospital partners to ensure timely diagnosis, advanced endoscopic procedures, and family-centered follow-up for children and adults.

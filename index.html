Why patients choose Al Farabi Center

We combine subspecialty expertise in adult and pediatric gastroenterology, hepatology, nutrition and endoscopy with coordinated, family-centered care.

Expert physicians & endoscopists

Consultant adult and pediatric gastroenterologists with international training and board certification, offering advanced diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopy.

On-site endoscopy suite

Upper endoscopy, colonoscopy, polypectomy, band ligation, foreign body retrieval and other procedures performed in a dedicated endoscopy suite for safe and efficient care.

Coordinated multidisciplinary team

Integrated internal medicine, pediatrics, surgery, dermatology, pharmacy and laboratory services to simplify referrals and follow-up for families.

Family-centered follow-up

Clear explanations, written plans and follow-up appointments tailored to each patient’s needs, with a focus on education and long-term health.

Clinic snapshot

A quick overview of the main services offered at Al Farabi Gastroenterology & Endoscopy Centre.

Adult gastroenterology & hepatology

Evaluation and management of reflux, ulcers, IBS, IBD, liver disease, biliary and pancreatic disorders.

Pediatric GI, liver & nutrition

Comprehensive care for infants, children and adolescents with digestive, liver, feeding and nutrition issues.

Endoscopy & urgent care

Endoscopic evaluation of bleeding, foreign body ingestion, variceal banding and other urgent GI conditions.

Allied services on site

Internal medicine, general pediatrics, general surgery, dermatology, pharmacy and laboratory services.

About Al Farabi Center

Al Farabi Gastroenterology & Endoscopy Center is a dedicated digestive health and multi-specialty clinic in Diyala, Iraq, affiliated with the Diyala Health Ministry. The center brings together adult and pediatric gastroenterology, general pediatrics, internal medicine, general surgery, dermatology, endoscopy, laboratory and pharmacy services.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, evidence-based gastroenterology and general medical care in a respectful, family-centred environment, with particular attention to the needs of children and adolescents requiring specialized GI services.

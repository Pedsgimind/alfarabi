About Al Farabi Gastroenterology & Endoscopy Centre

Al Farabi is a multidisciplinary digestive health centre in Baqubah, Diyala. We combine adult and pediatric gastroenterology, hepatology, nutrition, internal medicine, pediatrics, surgery, dermatology, pharmacy, and on-site endoscopy under one roof.

  • Integrated care for adults and children with digestive, liver, pancreatic, and nutrition disorders.
  • On-site endoscopy suite for urgent and elective procedures.
  • Close collaboration with Diyala Health Directorate and regional hospital partners.
Location Baqubah, Diyala
Specialties 7+
Emergency triage 24/7 phone / WhatsApp

Patient-centred care

Our model prioritizes clear explanations, written plans, and coordination with referring physicians. We aim to make advanced endoscopy and subspecialty care accessible within Diyala.

Our mission

To deliver safe, evidence-based gastroenterology and endoscopy services for adults and children in Diyala and the surrounding governorates, with a focus on quality, safety, and teaching.

Our vision

To become a regional reference centre for digestive health in Iraq, partnering with local hospitals, universities, and international centres to raise standards of endoscopy and pediatric GI care.

How we work

  • Structured clinic triage and appointment system.
  • Standardized documentation and electronic visit summaries.
  • Close communication with referring physicians and families.
  • Staged investigation plans to keep testing focused and affordable.

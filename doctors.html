Our Doctors

Al Farabi Centre brings together adult and pediatric gastroenterologists, hepatologists, general pediatricians, internal medicine physicians, surgeons and dermatologists, with shared commitment to safe, evidence-based care.

Pediatric GI

Dr. Mohammad Hussein, MD, FRCPC

Consultant Pediatric Gastroenterologist, Hepatologist & Nutrition Specialist

Canadian-trained pediatrician and pediatric gastroenterologist providing comprehensive digestive, liver and nutrition care for infants, children and adolescents.

  • Diagnostic and therapeutic pediatric upper endoscopy and colonoscopy.
  • Evaluation of chronic abdominal pain, IBD, celiac disease and GI bleeding.
  • Management of feeding difficulties, malnutrition and obesity.
Adult GI

Dr. A. Khazraji

Consultant Adult Gastroenterologist & Endoscopist

Adult gastroenterologist providing diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopy for a wide range of upper and lower GI disorders.

  • Evaluation of reflux, ulcers, anemia and GI bleeding.
  • Screening and surveillance colonoscopy for colorectal cancer.
  • Management of chronic liver disease and portal hypertension.

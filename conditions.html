Conditions We Commonly See

Patients are referred to Al Farabi for a wide range of digestive, liver and nutrition problems. The list below is not exhaustive but highlights common reasons for consultation in our adult and pediatric clinics.

Upper GI

Esophagus & Stomach

  • Reflux disease and heartburn.
  • Difficulty swallowing, food sticking, achalasia.
  • Peptic ulcer disease, H. pylori infection.
  • Unexplained nausea, vomiting, early satiety.
Lower GI

Small & Large Intestine

  • Chronic diarrhea or constipation.
  • Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
  • Inflammatory bowel disease (Crohn’s disease, ulcerative colitis).
  • Unexplained weight loss, anemia or GI bleeding.
Liver

Liver & Biliary Conditions

  • Viral hepatitis, fatty liver disease and cirrhosis.
  • Cholestasis and jaundice in infants and children.
  • Autoimmune and metabolic liver disorders.
  • Gallbladder stones and biliary colic.
Pancreas & Nutrition

Pancreatic & Nutrition Disorders

  • Acute and chronic pancreatitis.
  • Celiac disease and malabsorption.
  • Malnutrition, faltering growth, obesity.
  • Feeding difficulties, selective eating, enteral nutrition follow-up.
Pediatric

Common Pediatric Issues

  • Infant colic, reflux and cow’s milk protein allergy.
  • Chronic abdominal pain, constipation and encopresis.
  • GI manifestations of systemic diseases (rheumatologic, neurologic, genetic).
Dermatology & Other

Skin & Systemic Conditions

  • Skin disease linked to GI or liver conditions (e.g., psoriasis, pruritus).
  • Chronic urticaria and allergy-related rashes.
  • Secondary osteoporosis and vitamin deficiency states.
